VAL NG

Valerie Ng is a UX/UI designer, graphic designer, and artist residing in Boston. Originally from Malaysia, Val has lived all over the world, including Bali, Senegal, and London. During her time in Bali, Val volunteered for the very first TEDxUbud and has been pursuing key philosophies of TED ever since: design, technology, and ideas worth spreading.

Aside from art and technology, Val likes animals, red wine, and long motorcycle rides on open roads.
